Martin Kocour

I am a PhD student at the BUT Speech@FIT, part of the Department of Computer Graphics and Multimedia at Faculty of Information Technology at Brno University of Technology, where I work on speech recognition. My PhD advisors are Jan Cernocky and Lucas Ondel Young.

When I'm not immersed in my research, I enjoy spending time with my wife and friends or exploring the outdoors through hiking or skiing.
